AttysHRV

Your heart beats a bit faster when you are breathing in and a bit slower when you are breathing out. The more relaxed you are the more your heart-rate changes during breathing. However, this also works the other way around: you can use deep breathing to de-stress which in turn will change your heart-rate according to your breathing.

How to install

AttysHRV can be downloaded for free from the Oculus App Lab.

How to use

You need:

  • Oculus Quest 2
  • Attys

where the Attys transmits your heartbeats (ECG) to the Oculus Quest 2:

  1. Put the Attys in your pocket and connect its positive electrode to your left ankle (or hip), the negative one to the right shoulder and the ground electrode to the left shoulder. Make sure the Attys is paired with the Oculus Quest 2.
  2. Start AttysHRV.
  3. Deep breaths, allow yourself to relax and enjoy the soothing movement of the waves.
